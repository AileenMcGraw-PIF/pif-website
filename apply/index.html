Joining Presidential Innovation Fellows (PIF)

Thank you for your interest in the Presidential Innovation Fellows (PIF) program! We're excited that you're excited to jump into public service with confidence. At PIF, you'll never go it alone. As a cohort, PIF's community and collective knowledge are here to support every fellow. Are you excited about the opportuntiy to create profound impact without sacrificing your passions? Email pif-application@pif.gov with the subject line "PIF interest" to be notified when our applications open. Our application is currently closed. We typically accept applications on a yearly basis, from February through May. After the application deadline, we review applications, interviews select candidates on a rolling basis, and extend offers in late summer.

You can always learn more about our newest PIF cohort, all current Presidential Innovation Fellows, read about our projects with federal partners, and connect with us on social media! You can also learn more about the federal hiring process on the Join TTS website.
